# ToneCraft Pro - Standalone Ringtone Generator

You can find your standalone application files in: **`D:\standalone\`**

### Available Files:
1.  **`ToneCraft_Pro.exe`**: The portable standalone app. You can run this directly!
2.  **`ToneCraft_Pro_Installer.exe`**: The installer if you want to install it on your PC permanently.

### Key Features:
- **New Ringtone Button**: Click this to reset and make as many ringtones as you like without restarting.
- **Auto-Sync**: Connect your phone, enable USB debugging, and click "Craft" to send directly to your phone.
- **Optimized OGG**: Files are tiny (< 30KB) and high quality, perfect for Android.

Enjoy crafting your ringtones! 🚀
